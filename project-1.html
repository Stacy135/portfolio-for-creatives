Dine Out

Poster Design and Illustration

This three poster series promotes BC’s annual Dine Out event. Each poster highlights a different dining theme: global cuisines, kids' meals, and vegan options. Vibrant colours and lively treatment of different dishes and food types are mean to entice people try new dining experiences.

  • About Stacy

    I am an illustrator who loves using bright colours and simple lines to create lively, adorable characters and stories for both digital and print projects. I am also skilled at creating engaging illustrations that help businesses connect with their audiences. I moved to Canada in 2016 and graduated from Capilano University's IDEA School of Design in 2025. During my school I interned at the Suzhou Huachuang Art Museum in China. My illustration style also includes traditional Chinese subjects and elements. If you think my work might be a fit for your next project, please reach out and we can chat.
