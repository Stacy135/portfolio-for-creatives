Dine Out

Poster Design and Illustration

This three poster series promotes BC’s annual Dine Out event. Each poster highlights a different dining theme: global cuisines, kids' meals, and vegan options. Vibrant colours and lively treatment of different dishes and food types are mean to entice people try new dining experiences.

  • About Stacy

    I am an illustrator that loves using bright colours and lines to create lively, adorable characters and animals. I moved to Canada in 2016 and graduated from Capilano University's IDEA School of Design in 2025. I've interned at the Suzhou Huachuang Art Museum in China. My illustration styles include traditional Chinese and children's illustration. Please reach out if you enjoy my style.
