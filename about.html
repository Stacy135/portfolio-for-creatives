  • About me

    I'm an ENFP illustrator with a vibrant Chinese background. I moved to Canada in ninth grade as an international student, and I'm about to graduate from Capilano University's IDEA program. I've interned at the Suzhou Huachuang Art Museum, and I'm continuously learning to become a better illustrator.

    I'm skilled in various illustration styles, including traditional Chinese, children's illustrations, ACGN, flat design, and minimalism. I love using bright colours and lines to create lively, adorable characters and animals that bring joy and healing.

    My favourite clothing colour is black because it makes me look slim. Currently, I live with my cat and enjoy cooking—often inviting friends over for gatherings. My dream is to own a large house and have three cats and two dogs.

    I'm deeply passionate about drawing; it's not just a job for me. If you enjoy my style, please feel free to reach out for collaborations
